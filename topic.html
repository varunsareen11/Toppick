AGING

The subject of health is a recurrent one in many conversations, and for good reason, a person's health is affected by literally everything that happens to and around them. People of all ages are constantly talking about health, from older people discussing how aging has affected their wellbeing to young adults frequently discussing topics like mental health and skincare to children who are eternally awed and confused by how rapidly they're growing.
